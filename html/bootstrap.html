Albert Einstein

Born 14 March 1879
Place Ulm, Germany
Field Theoretical Physics
Known for Theory of Relativity

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

Einstein is best known for his famous equation E = mc², which describes the relationship between energy and mass.

Early Life

Einstein was born in Ulm in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire.

Career

He worked at the Swiss Patent Office before becoming a professor and developing many groundbreaking theories in physics.
